About John

John Patrick is currently studying at the University of the Philippines Open University. He has been working in Japan in the field of Software Quality Assurance for three years. Recently, his dedication to learning Japanese has led him to pass the Japanese Language Proficiency Test Level N2 exam, and is currently preparing for the most difficult level, the JLPT N1. He spends most of his free time listening to self-improvement audiobooks while simultaneously managing his daily chores. His favorite book is Atomic Habits, which taught him how a single habit can determine the trajectory of our lives.
